# Product Definition: NeoY's Hacker Podcast

## Initial Concept

An AI-powered Hacker News podcast system that automatically crawls daily top stories, summarizes them in Chinese, and generates audio via TTS.

## Target Audience

- **Chinese-speaking tech enthusiasts:** Individuals who want to stay updated on Hacker News trends through audio consumption in their native language.
- **Podcast listeners:** Users interested in daily tech summaries and deep-dives available on their favorite podcast platforms.

## Core Value Proposition

- **Time-saving Summarization:** Provides concise AI-generated Chinese summaries of complex English tech articles and discussions.
- **Convenient Audio Consumption:** Enables users to consume tech news while commuting or multitasking through a high-quality TTS experience.

## Key Features

- **Automated Content Pipeline:** A fully automated daily workflow that handles crawling, AI summarization, and TTS audio generation.
- **Multi-platform Access:** Availability via a dedicated web interface and standard RSS feeds for podcast applications.
- **On-Demand Episode Generation:** Allows users to input specific article URLs or sets of articles (similar to NotebookLM) to generate custom podcast episodes on demand.
